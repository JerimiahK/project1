ABOUT

Welcome to a Pastel Dreams. It is a place to experience a variety of art and a place to let your mind wander. We hope in the future to provide a place for people to be able to share all of their synthwave and vaporwave inspired creations. What starts as a dream can quickly be turned into a new idea, and a community that supports each other and helps inspire more creativity, like a fractal.
